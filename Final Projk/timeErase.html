King Crimson: Requiem

Skills

This section will detail the various skills of King Crimson: Requiem

Each skill can have different properties, damage outputs, and effects.

M1's (left clicks): Pretty basic move, throws one punch per click doing 9.7 damage at max Destructive Power.

E - Stand Barrage: A flurry of punches, doing 1.6 damage per hit w/ max Destructive

R - Stand Barrage Finisher: Heavy punch that ragdolls, dealing 19.3 damage

Y - Chop: KCR performs a bisecting Chop that deals 12.9 damage and 0.3 bleed (damage) over time

T - Epitaph (1.6 a hit): Teleports behind opponent if they attack you while Epitaph is activated. If fully upgraded, KCR performs "Raging Demon" (a reference to a Street Fighter move), hitting 20 heavy punches for 1.6 damage each, totaling 32 damage.

H - Dimensional Slash: KCR summons a scythe in the direction the screen is facing, crippling anyone hit for 32.2 damage

X - Time Skip: Teleports instantly, that's it.

Z - Time Erase: KCR erases time, creating a separate space. The red highlight indicates where someone was before time was erased. After time is up or manually deactivated, people teleport back to where they were.

Recommended spec: Vampirism

Vampirism allows you to have faster regen, and hyper regen when your blood bar isn't empty, along with a counter.

Playstyle:

KCR is a very aggressive stand, focusing heavily on M1s (punching) with a mix of powerful abilities.

Combos:

Combo 1:(lowkey just spam anything close ranged) Time erase -> superhuman barrage + stingy eyes-> chop + m1s -> counter bait-> Dimensional Slash
Combo 2: Counter -> Dimensional slash + stingy eyes -> m1s + superhuman barrage -> time erase + chop -> epitaph bait
