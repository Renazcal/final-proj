Basic Character Tree points

Character Tree

Traits

This section will show you what points to put in into each spec and character tree build.

Each skill can have different properties, damage outputs, and effects.

Vitality - Health/p>

Agility - Running Speed

Sturdiness - reduces the amount of attacks that can stand crash you and your time to get up from ragdoll

Worthiness - Improves chances of not failing a stand arrow or rib cage, 2 Worthhy for arrows, 5 for rib cages and evolution items

Recommended Spec: Vamp

Vampirism allows you to have faster regen, and hyper regen when your blood bar isnt empty, along with a counter.

Upgrades and skills

Ultraviolet Resistance - buffs defense against hamon and burn damage from the sun, at max upgrade you lose the sun weakness

Blood Manipulation - you gain more blood from life drain and other attacks.

Hyper Regeneration - drains less blood when passive renegerating

Superhuman Strength - buffs your damage and the amount you heal from superhuman barrage, also buffs other attack statuses

Z - Life drain: a blockable grab that does 0.3 damage a tic and gives you a ton of blood. Set to B

C - Superhuman Barrage: a barrage doing 0.9 damage every tic and 16 damage at the end, healing for 16 and giving blood. Set to V

X - Space Ripper Stingy Eyes: ranged projectile, if super human strength is upgraded twice, you catch people on fire with this. Set to N

V - Vaporization Freeze: counter that freezes the opponent doing 36 damage and a slow down, gives blood. Set to M

Recommended Spec: Hamon

On maxed upgrades, gives 15% damage buff to all moves, and 22% more defense

Upgrades and skills

Hamon punch - Buffs damage

Lung capacity - Keeps your bar filled for longer

Your Next Line is!: If you block or parry an attack, you will taunt the opponent and lower their defense by 20% -

Secret Technique - when under 20% health you gain a speed boost to run away

Z - Clacker Boomerang: throw 2 hamon infused clackers dealing 9.2 damage each hit and can do more damage if the opponent is hit when they return, cancellable and blockable, stuns. Set to B.

X - Clacker Volley: cancellable, bring out 2 hamon infused clackers and beat the person infront of you with them doing 4.6 every hit. Set to N

C - Rebuff Overdrive: hit the other person with your elbows doing 27 damage and block break, keep as C

V - Tommygun Barrage: pull out a tommy gun and do a barrage doing 1.6 damage a tic, set to M if using.

G - Hamon Breathing: charges up your hamon bar, set to V

Recommended Spec: Spin

Vampirism allows you to have faster regen, and hyper regen when your blood bar isnt empty, along with a counter.

Upgrades and skills

Spin Duration - Makes the spin bar drain slower

Spin Charge - Charges your spin faster

Golden Skin - Increases defense by 5 each upgrade when spin is charged.

Spin Mastery - increases the damage spin attacks do when spin is charged, each upgrade is +20%.

G - The Golden Spin: charges your spin bar, Set to V

Z - Steel Ball: Throw a singular steel ball towards your cursor does 8.2 on the first hit and another 8.2 when it rebounds and IF it hits the opponent again. Set to N

X - Double Steel Ball: Throw two steel balls with similar function as the singular ball, high stun and does 6.8 per hit instead. Set to B

V - Whirling Steel Ball: Similar function, however throws a singular ball which on impact, creates an aoe tornado that does 34 damage, block breaks and stand crashes. Set to M

C - Twisting Spin Punch: Infuses your arm with spin and if you land the initial punch(does 8.2) and block breaks, does 21.8 when knocking away the target. Keep as C.
