Weather Report

Skills

This section will detail the various skills of Weather Report.

Each skill can have different properties, damage outputs, and effects.

M1's (left clicks): Pretty basic move, throws one punch per click doing 7 damage.

E - Stand Barrage: A flurry of punches, doing 1.2 damage per hit.

R - Stand Barrage Finisher: Heavy punch that ragdolls, dealing 9 damage.

T - Soaring Tempest: WR summons a tornado that does 25 damage on impact, blockable.

Y - Hazardous Forecast: Summons an electrical orb in front of you doing 36 damage, blockable.

G - Heavy Weather: WR liberates rain and rainbows, an AoE attack, that if in the range and touched by a rainbow, turns people into snails and lowers their defense by 20.

H - Atmospheric Manipulation: Summon an air pocket around you that slows down people and reflects any damage blocked.

X - Ice Spikes: Summon a wall of spikes that ragdoll enemies and does 17.5 damage and bleed if fully upgraded.

Z - Frog Rain: WR summons a rain of poisonous frogs that deal 1.2 each on impact and 2.2 poison damage.

Recommended spec: Vampirism

Vampirism allows you to have faster regen, and hyper regen when your blood bar isn't empty, along with a counter.

Playstyle:

WR is a very neutral stand, being highly defense-based and requires block breaks to perform combos.

Combos:

Combo 1:(While M1 trading) Super human barrage → Hazardous forecast → Frog rain + dash → Ice spikes → Stingy eye
Combo 2: Counter -> Hazardous Forecast + Ice spikes -> frog rain -> stingy eyes + superhuman barrage
