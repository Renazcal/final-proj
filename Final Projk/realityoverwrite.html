The World: Over Heaven

The World: Over Heaven, AKA TW:OH/TWOH, is known for its outrageous amount of damage it can do to you in the span of 5 seconds if caught off guard...

Skills

This section will detail the various skills of The World: Over Heaven.

Each skill can have different properties, damage outputs, and effects.

M1's (left clicks): Basic move, throws one punch per click doing 10.1 damage at max Destructive Power.

E - Stand Barrage: A flurry of punches, doing 1.6 damage per hit.

R - Stand Barrage Finisher: Heavy punch that ragdolls, dealing 19.3 damage.

T - Knife Throw: With max upgrades, throws 3 knives that deal 8.1 damage each with an AOE stun. AOE stun acts as a 4th knife, dealing another 8.1 damage.

Y - Reality Overwriting Punch: Deals 20.1 damage and overwrites the person it's used on, crippling and stunning them. Blockable.

U and X - Injury Overwrites: With max Midas Touch, U heals you for 52, and X heals another for 52.

H - Time Stop: Stops time for 5–9 seconds based on the upgrade. Time stop damage is nerfed to 1/3 of an opponent's health.

Z - Time Skip: Teleport move, that's it.

G - Heaven Ascended Smite: Twoh uses lightning from the heavens to strike people, dealing 30 damage. Block breaks, cannot be parried but can be cancelled.

Recommended spec: Joseph Joestar's Hamon

Hamon increases your base damage. Combined with TWOH's true damage passive, you'll deal around 15.1 per M1, shredding through other specs like Boxing, Spin, Pluck, etc.

Playstyle:

TWOH + Joseph is a random move throw playstyle, but you can chain into certain things. Aggressive playstyle.

Combos:

Combo 1: (Hamon Charged) Clacker boomerangs → dash → 3x M1s → clacker volley → 2 M1s + knives → overwrite → smite
Combo 2: (Hamon Charged) Clacker boomerangs → dash → 3x M1s → clacker volley → 2 M1s + knives → rebuff overdrive → overwrite + smite → time stop (M1 as many times as possible)
